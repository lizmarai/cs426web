            <a href="../finals/2018-Spring/Videos/qwopbo.mp4">QWOPBO</a> <br />
            Group: Abdulaziz Malik, Sarah Ather, Edward Reyes (CS)<br />
            Players play as boxers in a special league for people who are
            recovering quadriplegics and the player want to be number one in the
            league because he used to be a pro. Using QWOP style mechanics,
            players must control the movement of the boxer’s arms and legs to
            make the character move around and make moves. At the same time,
            they have to avoid falling over or getting hit.
