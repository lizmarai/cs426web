              href="../finals/2025-Spring/Videos/1_HaveYouTriedTurningItOffandOnAgain.mp4"
              >Have You Tried Turning It Off and On Again </a
            ><br />
            Group: Matthew Mohaupt, Scott Hong, MaryJo Santos
            <br />
            The main idea of the game is trying to do IT work during a blackout,
            where players will have to solve IT hardware and software related
            issues for one work week, gain money to upgrade supplies and try to
            earn as much money as possible by the end of the week. Though
            unfortunately the power is shaky and rolling blackouts occur
            regularly and any software related issues cannot be solved when the
            power goes out. It is be designed to work for the platform: x86_64
            Microsoft Windows 10 and above.
